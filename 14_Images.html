HTML Images s

Images will make webpage more beautiful. 

    <img src="image local or web location" alt="This is my logo" style="width:150px; height: 150px">

you can also use width or height attribute like this: 
    <img src="image location"alt="This is my logo" width="100" height="100">

Personally recommend use style="width height". It will not overwrite by CSS.
