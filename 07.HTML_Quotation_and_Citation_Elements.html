Quotations

Short Quotations

HTML use <q> define short quotation. 

    <p>Tom says:<q>How are you today?</q></p>

Tom says:How are you today?

Abbreviations

HTML use <abbr> tag defines an abbreviations, like "UFO" 

    look at the <abbr title="Unidentified Flying Object">UFO</abbr>,
    its unidentified flying object.

look at the UFO, its unidentified flying object.

Address

HTML <address> tag defines contact information. In browsers, they are italic and browsers will add line break before and after <address> element. 

<address>
    Name: zsjng. <br>
    Address: 1st_street. <br>
    TEL: 123456789. <br>
</address>
Name: zsjng.
Address: 1st_street.
TEL: 123456789.
